Marin Method Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026.

Developer and Contact

Marin Method is provided by Black Crow Labs.

Privacy contact: support@blackcrowlabs.com.

Data We Collect

  • Account data: email address, display name, password hash, invite/access code state, authentication sessions, and account settings.
  • Optional profile data: profile photo if the user chooses to upload one.
  • Training data: selected voice range, selected default songs, lesson and practice attempts, scores, timestamps, progress history, and calendar activity.
  • Audio analysis data: microphone input is used during tuner, lesson, and practice features to estimate pitch and evaluate singing exercises. The app may store or sync derived pitch, score, and timing data needed to provide the training experience.
  • Device and calibration data: audio route, device model, and calibration settings may be used to keep visual guides and sound playback synchronized.
  • Cached content: lesson instructions, exercise definitions, and song assets may be downloaded and stored on the device so the app can work when the network or server is unavailable.

How We Use Data

We use the data to authenticate users, run singing lessons and practice tools, score exercises, show progress, sync activity across sessions, provide audio calibration, and maintain app reliability.

We do not sell personal or sensitive user data.

Sharing and Security

Data is sent to Marin Method servers over HTTPS. We may use service providers needed to host, operate, store, and secure the app. We do not share personal data for advertising.

Retention and Deletion

Users can request account deletion inside the Android app from Settings, or outside the app at the account deletion page. Account deletion removes login, profile, schedule, progress, lesson session, and practice session records tied to the account. Shared lesson library content is not deleted.

Outside-app deletion page: https://marinlafare.github.io/marin_method/account-deletion.html.

Children

Marin Method is not directed to children under 13. If we learn that a child under 13 provided personal data without appropriate consent, we will delete that data.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app changes. The updated date at the top of this page shows when the policy was last changed.
