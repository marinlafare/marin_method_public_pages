Marin Method Account Deletion

Use this page to request deletion of a Marin Method app account and account-associated data.

Delete From Inside The App

Open the Android app, go to Settings, choose delete account, and confirm. The app will remove account-bound data and sign you out.

Delete Without The App

Email support@blackcrowlabs.com with the subject Marin Method account deletion.

Include the email address used for the Marin Method account. We may ask for extra information only to verify that the request belongs to the account.

What Is Deleted

Deletion removes the app account, login sessions, fun-user profile, schedule, song defaults, lesson progress, practice progress, lesson run records, and practice run records tied to the account.

Shared lesson libraries, generated exercise templates, and public lesson song catalog entries are not account data and are not deleted.
